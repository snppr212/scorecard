Balls in play against our pitchers

Every batted ball the opposing teams put in play against our pitchers, organized by pitcher, with where it went, trajectory (grounder / line / fly / pop), and how hard it was hit. Strikeouts, walks & HBP are excluded (no ball in play). Pulled from all finalized games — contact tags (location / type / hardness) are entered when you score a ball in play, so they don't need pitch-by-pitch logging. Each pitcher shows tag coverage so you know how complete the contact detail is. Read-only.
