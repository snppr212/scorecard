Strip phantom 3rd-out advances

When the at-bat that makes the 3rd out also moved runners up (e.g. a groundout where trailing runners advance), the app was recording those as real advancesOnAB — but the inning's over, so the advance is meaningless. This walks every finalized game, finds the AB that recorded each half-inning's 3rd out, and clears its advancesOnAB. Runs (pscored) and the out itself are left untouched. Preview first.

Innings that ended on the 6-run cap (not 3 outs) are not touched — advances there are legit.
