Retrofit pitch codes across every non-fully-logged game

Pitch code key

CodeMeaningCounts as
BBallball
SSwinging strike (batter swung & missed)strike, swing
LLooking strike (called strike, no swing)strike
FFoul (batter contacted, ball went foul)strike, swing
FTFoul tip (caught by catcher — can end AB on a 2-strike count)strike, swing
XBall in play (batter put it in play)strike, swing
HHBP (pitch hit the batter — off the plate)ball + HBP
UUnknown pitch (real pitch but we don't know what type — used for retrofitting non-logged ABs where the pitch could have been B/S/L/F/FT)nothing — total only
UsUnknown strike — we KNOW it was a strike (because of BB invariant: all non-ball pitches in a BB must be strikes) but we don't know which type (S/L/F/FT)strike only

Retrofit rules

For games NOT marked as pitch-by-pitch logged, walks every AB and rewrites the pitch sequence under these rules (matching the e2 work we did earlier):

  K  → [U × (N-3), Us, Us, S]              — N-3 unknowns + 2 unknown-strikes + final swinging strike
  KL → [U × (N-3), Us, Us, L]              — same, but the K-ending pitch was looking
  BB → [Us × (N-4), B, B, B, B]            — every non-ball MUST be a strike (BB invariant)
  HBP → [U × (N-1), H]                     — N-1 unknowns + the HBP pitch
  BIP (1B/2B/3B/HR/LLHR/GO/FO/LO/PO/FF/SAC/FC/DP/GIDP/TP/ROE/CI/AO) → [U × (N-1), X]
  N = ab.pc (total pitch count)
