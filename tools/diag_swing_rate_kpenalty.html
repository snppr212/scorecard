Swing-rate K-penalty diagnostic

Replaces the flat v2.37 K penalty (−0.7·kRate) with one driven by each kid's actual swing behavior in fully pitch-logged games — not just the last pitch of a strikeout. Contact swings (X / F / FT = ball-in-play, foul, foul tip) earn full credit as good swings; whiffs (S) earn partial credit (a swing, but missed); called strikes (L = watching strike three go by) earn nothing. A kid who swings the bat and makes contact gets the penalty eased; a kid who stands and watches gets it harsher. kRate is still computed from ALL games (recency-weighted); only the per-kid swing profile comes from fully-logged pitch data.
