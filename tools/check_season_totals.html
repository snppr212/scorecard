Season totals diagnostic

Compares the league-site season totals you pasted against the sum of per-outing data we already pushed to appdata/oppPitcherLog.

Legend: ✓ match — outing sum matches season total · ⚠ off — mismatch (shows delta) · + new — pitcher in season totals but no outings logged (will be added fresh) · ?? extra — pitcher has outings but isn't in season totals (probable name typo to investigate)

IP is intentionally ignored (the league IP data is incomplete for most teams).
