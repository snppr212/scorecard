Pitches per out — our staff

P/Out = pitches thrown ÷ outs recorded. Lower is better (efficient). Fewer pitches per out = quick innings; high P/Out = laboring, deep counts, baserunners. The season totals (Sections 1–2) use the app's IP definition (batter outs: K, groundout, flyout, etc. — matches the season pitching page exactly). Section 3 walks every half-inning we pitched and flags the ones that ended without 3 outs — the run-limit / called / walk-off innings where the bats kept coming — counting all outs (incl. runners thrown out on the bases).
