Audit runs vs RBI — Mighty Mussels season

Pulls every finalized game from Firestore, walks the ABs in order while simulating the base state, and identifies missed-RBI cases. Read-only.
What counts as an RBI: a runner scoring on a hit, a sacrifice fly, a bases-loaded BB/HBP/CI, or most outs that drive in a run (FC, GO, FO can all earn an RBI). What doesn't: steal of home, error, wild pitch / passed ball, or the batter himself scoring on his own home run (well — he does get the RBI for himself, that's already credited).
