K vs KL split diagnostic — fully pitch-logged games only

For each Mussel, splits strikeouts into K (swinging) vs KL (looking) — gated to games with fullPitchLog === true so the classification is trustworthy (in fully-logged games the K vs KL flag is auto-derived from whether the 3rd strike was an S or L pitch code). Shows current penalty (−0.7·(K+KL)/PA) vs proposed penalty (−0.5·K/PA − 1.0·KL/PA), and a full AB-by-AB list of every strikeout with its pitch sequence so you can verify the classification. Read-only.
